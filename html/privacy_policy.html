Cookies

Our website uses so-called “cookies”. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. They can be temporary (session cookies) or permanent. Session cookies are automatically deleted after your visit ends. Permanent cookies remain on your device until you delete them or your browser does so automatically.


  • Cookies can be:
  • First-party (set by us)
  • Third-party (set by external services, e.g. payment providers, videos)
  • Some cookies are technically necessary; others are used to analyze user behavior or for advertising purposes.
  • Necessary cookies are stored based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR, unless another legal basis is stated.
